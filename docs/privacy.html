BodyWeather — Privacy Policy

Published by Anca Stancut

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Overview

BodyWeather is a personal wellness tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

BodyWeather collects wellness data from two sources:

Manual input:

  • Sleep hours and sleep quality
  • Energy level
  • Hydration level
  • Stress level
  • Language preference
  • Notification settings

Apple Health (HealthKit):

With your explicit permission, BodyWeather reads sleep data from Apple Health. This includes data from connected devices such as Apple Watch, Whoop, Oura, Garmin, and others that sync with Apple Health. This data is used solely to pre-fill your daily sleep hours automatically. BodyWeather only reads sleep analysis data and does not write any data to Apple Health. The app works fully without Apple Health access — you can always enter data manually instead.

Health data read from Apple Health is:

  • Stored only on your device — never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Never used for advertising, marketing, or data mining
  • Never sold, shared, or disclosed to anyone
  • Used exclusively to calculate your daily wellness score

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device only. BodyWeather does not have servers, does not transmit your data over the internet, and does not share your data with any third parties. Your wellness data never leaves your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

BodyWeather does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage statistics, device identifiers, or any form of telemetry.

No Account Required

BodyWeather does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in. The app works entirely offline.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all BodyWeather data at any time by uninstalling the app. No data remains on any server because no data is ever sent to one.

Children's Privacy

BodyWeather does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
